How to Create Your Pookalam

  1. Select a flower type from the options (round, star, small, or petal)
  2. Choose a color for your flower
  3. Click on the canvas to place the flower
  4. Create patterns by placing flowers in symmetric designs
  5. Use the "Add Template" button to get started with a basic design
  6. Reset the canvas if you want to start over
  7. Save your design when you're finished

Traditional Pookalams are often circular and symmetric. Try creating patterns with radial symmetry for an authentic look!
